About the event

A divine call to
a new season

Youth Congress 2026 is a two-day spirit-filled gathering for young believers ready to encounter God, discover purpose, and be ignited for a life of impact.

01

The theme

"A New Beginning" (Rev 21:5) — God's invitation to every young person to shed the old and boldly step into His fresh purpose.

02

Who should attend

Young people aged 13–35 who are hungry for God, seeking direction, or simply want to grow in faith and community.

03

What to expect

Anointed preaching, deep worship, powerful prayer, peer fellowship, and life-defining divine encounters.

04

Completely free

No cost to attend. Registration is free and all sessions are open to everyone. Come ready to be transformed!
